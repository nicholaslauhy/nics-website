    • IDEATE

    Aug 2024 - Sept 2024

    IDEATE is a competition organised by the Innovation and Design Programme (IDP) Department, where the theme was to create something that can be implemented to future homes in Singapore. Our team of 5 invented Laun-DRY, an automated awning system that helps to shelter your clothes from the rain, hoping to target those living in HDBs which fill up the majority of Singapore's population.

    We had to apply everything that we learnt and put into practice. Being the only member with an engineering background, I was in charge of the product design and conceptualising the system to be used. This was with the help of our student mentor, Year 3 Mechanical Engineering Student Thulusidas, and current NTU professor and former Vice-President of SIA Engineering, Professor Lim Yeow Khee.

    Out of 60 teams, we managed to emerge as finalists but were not able to secure a podium position. Laun-DRY remains an ongoing project that we plan to expand on.

    Skills required:

  • Fusion360 CAD
  • Innovation and Design
  • Project Management
